FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need prior Linux experience?

+

No, our course starts from the absolute basics. Module 1 covers all the fundamental Linux concepts you need to progress to more advanced topics. Complete beginners are welcome!

Is this course legal and ethical?

+

Absolutely. We teach ethical hacking techniques for educational purposes only. We emphasize the importance of only using these skills on systems you own or have explicit permission to test. The course includes guidance on legal and ethical considerations.

What hardware/software do I need?

+

You'll need a computer with at least 8GB RAM and 50GB free disk space. We recommend using a virtual machine setup. The course provides detailed instructions for installing all required software, including Kali Linux and virtualization tools.

How do I get support if I'm stuck?

+

After unlocking the full course by following our Instagram, you'll gain access to our private community where you can ask questions and get help from both instructors and fellow students. We also offer direct support via email for complex issues.

How long does it take to complete the course?

+

The complete course contains over 50 hours of content. Most students take 2-3 months to complete it while practicing the techniques. You can progress at your own pace, and you'll have lifetime access to all materials and updates.
